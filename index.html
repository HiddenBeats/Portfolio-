All About Me

My name is Michael, and I am 13 years old. I am someone who enjoys learning new things and expressing myself creatively. Two of my biggest passions are coding and making music. They may seem very different, but to me, they both let me create something unique from scratch.

I started getting interested in coding a couple of years ago. I love how you can build anything from games to websites just by typing lines of code. It feels like solving a puzzle, and it's exciting to see everything come together and work. Coding teaches me to think logically and not give up when things don’t work right the first time.

Besides coding, I also enjoy making music. I like experimenting with different sounds and creating beats or melodies that match how I’m feeling. Music is a way for me to relax, have fun, and be creative in a different way than coding.

In the future, I hope to combine my interests—maybe by creating music apps or video games with original soundtracks. I know I still have a lot to learn, but I’m excited to keep growing and discovering new things.
